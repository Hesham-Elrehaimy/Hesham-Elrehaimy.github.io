Welcome to the Rafael Nadal fan club

Information About Rafael Nadal:

  • Was born in Mallorca, Spain on June, 3 1986.
  • Turned Pro in 2001.
  • He's naturally right handed, but his uncle made him play tennis with his left hand.
  • His first grand slam was Roland Garros 2005.
  • He won the olympic gold medal for singles in 2008 and for doubles in 2016.
  • He is coached by his uncle Toni Nadal
  • He was ranked #1 for the first time in 2008

For more information visit Rafael Nadal's profile on the ATP World Tour website.
